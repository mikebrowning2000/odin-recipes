Chicken Salad

Description

Chicken Salad is a meal that I can eat any time of day. I like to stray a bit from the original recipe when I make it.

Ingredients

  • 1 boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1/2 lb thick cut bacon
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbs dill pickle cubes
  • 1/2 cup chopped scallions
  • 1 cup mayo (Homemade is best, but Hellmann's is good as well.)
  • 1 packet of Hidden Valley Ranch seasoning

Directions

  1. Cook chicken breast any way you like to an internal temp of 165F and let cool in the fridge until cold.
  2. Finely chop the chicken breast and add to bowl.
  3. Add the remaining ingredients and combine.
  4. Serve with bread or club crackers and enjoy!
