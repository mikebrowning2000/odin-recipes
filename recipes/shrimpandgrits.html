Shrimp and Grits

Description

One of my favorite dishes for a quick lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of grits
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 cups of cheddar cheese
  • 1 lb raw, peeled, and deveined shrimp
  • 1/2 lb thick cut bacon
  • 1 cup chopped scallions for garnish

Directions

  1. Grits

    1. Add water to a boiler on high heat until at a rolling boil.
    2. Add grits and reduce heat to med and stir often.
    3. Once thickened add cheese, stir well, and remove from heat.

  2. Shrimp

    1. Preheat skillet on med-high heat and add your favorite cooking oil.
    2. Add shrimp to skillet and season with salt and black pepper.
    3. Cook for 2-3 mins making sure to flip about half way through.

  3. Bacon

    1. Preheat skillet on med-high heat.
    2. Chop bacon into roughly 1/2" pieces and add to skillet.
    3. Cook bacon until crispy.

  4. Plating

    1. Add a generous ladle of grits into a bowl.
    2. Add shrimp to your heart's content.
    3. Garnish with chopped scallions and bacon.
    4. Enjoy!
